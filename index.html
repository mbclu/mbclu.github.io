The Write Track

A train themed handwriting game designed for toddlers

About the Game

This is a simple game designed to demonstrate how to draw letters. Each letter scene will present a demonstration by moving the train along the letter track. Try to draw the letter on your own by touching and dragging the train.

How to Play

Tap anywhere on the title screen to get started. Tapping ABC will allow the you to choose which letter you would like to practice. Tapping the left or right arrows will move to the next or previous letter. Tap and hold to start moving the train around on the track. Collect all of the envelopes to complete each letter. When you are done the next letter will automatically load.
